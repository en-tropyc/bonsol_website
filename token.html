Token Specifications

Supply: 21,000,000 tokens

Decimals: 9

Distribution

Fair Launch: 60%

Guiding Principles

No VCs or Airdrops

  • No fundraising or investors
  • No pre-sale or pre-allocation, except for initial contributors (5%)

Transparency

  • Full disclosure of token information before launch
  • Details on utility, supply, and roadmap to be made public

Open Participation

  • Equal opportunity for all to acquire tokens at launch
  • No preferential treatment for any participants

Bonsol Objectives

  • Fair Pricing
  • Quality of Service
  • Decentralization

Token Utility

Staking and Tiered Access

  • Producers stake BON to participate in the network
  • Higher stakes unlock access to more intensive workloads
  • Tiered staking system ensures fair distribution of tasks

Proof Fees and Incentives

  • Consumers pay fees in BON for proof generation
  • Early adopters benefit from proof discounts
  • Tips incentivize timely and accurate proof delivery

Network Fee Sharing

  • BON stakers earn pro-rata share of network fees
  • Encourages long-term alignment with protocol
